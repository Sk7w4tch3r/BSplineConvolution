BSpline Convolution Interface

Instructions

  • Click to add a point, and drag to move it.
  • Right click on a point to delete it, or on a blank space in each panel to remove all points.
  • Change the BSpline degree by up/down arrow keys, make sure that the degree is less than number of control points.
  • Slide the kernel over the signal to demonstrate the convolution result with left/right arrow keys
